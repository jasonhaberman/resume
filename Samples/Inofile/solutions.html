ChartMD From consent forms, referrals, lab results, faxes and clinical notes, to an entire patient file, Inofile ChartMD delivers a fast and effective solution for handling unstructured content in healthcare. These critical pieces of patient information are challenging to capture and store for future accessibility. ChartMD takes the guesswork out of completing a patient's narrative in the digital space.

At the touch of a few buttons, patient documents can be organized and labeled for future searches. Insert page breaks, rotate pages, and sort by document type, patient name, or visit date, whichever is most useful and relevant. All features and options help to cut capture costs by 50-75%.
