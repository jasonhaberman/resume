About Inofile

Inofile is about capturing clinical documents, streamlining workflows and providing access to valuable information. Our distinct product offerings structure and exchange interoperable healthcare documents, enhancing the effectiveness of patient care.

In any healthcare environment, completing patient records and increasing quality of care should be top of mind. Inofile is committed to bringing substantial change to the way providers manage patient information and integrate with legacy workflows. Inofile's lightweight, affordable software solutions solve the most perplexing issues in chart completion and allow caregivers to refocus attention on patients.

Combining the right technology with a customer experience that is easy to implement and simple to use improves clinical workflow, lowering cost and elevating efficiency. Our unique approach to unstructured information is continually evolving with the support of leading manufacturers, standard groups and industry experts.
