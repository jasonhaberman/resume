Partners

Embedded Solutions Inofile serves as a technology partner, offering complete software solutions for some of the world's largest hardware providers in healthcare. Strategic partnership with Ricoh and Fujitsu means clinics and hospitals are served up a user-friendly interface and functionality, out of the box, that integrates paper-based workflows into an increasingly digital environment. At any stage of Meaningful Use compliance, Inofile solutions offer answers to the most difficult challenge in chart digitization: capture and preservation of unstructured content. Integration with existing workflows is a snap, and valuable patient data is stored securely, for future access.

EMR Partners Inofile's growing list of EMR partners enables our unstructured document solutions to integrate with more EMRs across the healthcare continuum, every day. A seamless process for getting unstructured content to your EMR encourages wide adoption of your technology and increases completion of digital patient charts. We offer software solutions, specific to the needs of individual EMRs. If you are an EMR vendor who would like to discuss Inofile's solution for unstructured content, please contact info@inofile.com
