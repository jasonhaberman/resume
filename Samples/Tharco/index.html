Stock Box Pioneer

In 1951, Barney Thacker, Scotty Harris and Hector Aitchison, three veterans of the container industry, formed Tharco in Southern California. Within a year, Aitchison had bought out his partners and was selling boxes out of the family barn in San Leandro, California.
