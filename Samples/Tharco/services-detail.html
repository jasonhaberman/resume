We serve and protect by design

Tharco designs custom packaging for products that are as big as a car, as small as a pencil, and 20 times more fragile than an egg. Using cutting-edge software tools, designers are able to create 3-D product models and then subject them to detailed analysis, a process that reduces overall development time and results in a superior final product.

Once the packaging design has been approved by the customer, samples are created and tested at Tharco’s extensive in-house lab. Fragility ratings and maximum drop height help to ensure that packaged products remain safe from damage due to handling, vibration and temperature variation.

"Our first priority as designers is to protect the product," John Cornell, Global Design Manager, Tharco
